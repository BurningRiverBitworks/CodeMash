So, is this session for you?...

If you're completely new to Android, ABSOLUTELY!

We'll cover the Android basics and help you to understand the foundational concepts needed to build yourself and Android app.

If you're a seasoned Android vet - we'd love to hack on some code with you so feel free to join. The material is geared towards those completely new to Android though so feel free to use the law of two feet - we won't be offended :)

We'd better get started!...

There's a few things you're going to need to do before arriving at the pre-compiler. PLEASE take the time to do them! We'll be on a tight timeline and we'll struggle if you dont.

Before Coming...

  • Prep your dev environment: It's BYOE (bring your own environment) so be sure what you bring is beefy enough to run Android Studio. The system requirements can be found here. Be sure that you have the Java Development Kit (JDK) installed as well. JDK7 can be found here.
  • Download Android Studio: We'll be using Android Studio. Be sure to donwload the latest version for your platform. We'll be expecting you to come with Android Studio installed and ready to rock. If you have any problems, feel free to hit me up on Twitter: @DaveShah or email me at david.a.shah@gmail.com and I'll do what I can to help.
  • Download the SDK packages and Emulator: Additional package installation instructions can be found here. Once you get to step 5 of the previous link, you should be all set and ready to go for the pre-compiler! Again, if you run into any snags, just hit me up on Twitter: @DaveShah or email me at david.a.shah@gmail.com and I'll do what I can to help, but please, do this before coming!
  • Setup your Google Play Developer account: Lastly, if you plan on publishing your app to the Google Play Store, be sure to sign up On The Google Play Developer Site. There's a one time $25.00 registration fee and once you're signed up, you can publish for life. Woot!
